8. Recommendations

Based on Testing:

  1. Base Color: Keep teal - it's neutral and works with all outline colors
  2. Grace Transitions: Use dashed lines in Sankey bands to indicate grace notes
  3. Pattern Dots: Use colored dots (6-8 colors max) for major pattern categories
  4. Static Glows: Match outline/pattern color - computationally cheap
  5. Audio Only: Reserve pulsing animation exclusively for audio playback
  6. Outline Usage:
    • Red/Orange/Yellow: Tonal modifiers
    • Blue/Green: Pattern selections
    • Purple/Pink: Special emphasis
    • Grey/Black: Inactive or muted
